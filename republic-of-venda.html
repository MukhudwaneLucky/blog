Republic of Venda

  • Honourable Bafana Mtileni
  • Daniel Marvern (Twitter)
  • March 25, 2022

To those who don't understand the picture below,That was a board showing Republic of Venda and South Africa



Venda from 1979-1994 was a republic which was ruled by a noble man President Patrick Mphephu from 1979-1988 and frank Ravele from 1988-1990. The Republic of venda motto was "shumela venda" meaning "always aspire for venda"

Venda was declared self-governing in 1973 and the venda Republic built an Airport,a venda bank in venda, a university in 1982 which is Univen,colleges were built and also a stadium was built not to forget industries which created multiple jobs.

The whole motive of my story is the fact that ever since the independent state of the Venda Republic which had its own president was dissolved in 1994, thing's after 1994 started to fall apart with greed and corruption.

The ANC led government collapsed everything created in the venda Republic during it's existence, take a look at the university of venda for an example,things are not working out properly there is corruption, incompetence and greed amongst the institution.

I Honourable Bafana Mtileni regardless of me not being a Venda salutes the Venda Republic, Venda was better before the Venda Republic was dissolved. All those living in Venda must protect the Venda legacy, Venda la ha tshikamuroho.
